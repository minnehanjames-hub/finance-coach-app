Economic Indicators

What to look for

Inflation

Rising prices can reduce purchasing power and influence interest-rate decisions.

Jobs

Low unemployment often signals a strong labor market, but it can also affect wage pressure.

Rates

Higher rates usually make borrowing more expensive and can pressure asset prices.

Growth

GDP tracks total economic output and helps show whether the economy is expanding.
